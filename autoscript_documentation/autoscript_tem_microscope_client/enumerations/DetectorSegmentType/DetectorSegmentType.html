DetectorSegmentType

Specifies scanning detector segment types.

Values

ALL "All" All segments present on the detector.
INNER1 "Inner1" The first segment of the inner ring.
INNER2 "Inner2" The second segment of the inner ring.
INNER3 "Inner3" The third segment of the inner ring.
INNER4 "Inner4" The fourth segment of the inner ring.
INNER_RING "InnerRing" All segments present in the inner ring: INNER1, INNER2, INNER3 and INNER4.
OUTER1 "Outer1" The first segment of the outer ring.
OUTER2 "Outer2" The second segment of the outer ring.
OUTER3 "Outer3" The third segment of the outer ring.
OUTER4 "Outer4" The fourth segment of the outer ring.
OUTER_RING "OuterRing" All segments present in the outer ring: OUTER1, OUTER2, OUTER3 and OUTER4.
SEGMENT1 "Segment1" The first segment of the detector without rings.
SEGMENT2 "Segment2" The second segment of the detector without rings.
SEGMENT3 "Segment3" The third segment of the detector without rings.
SEGMENT4 "Segment4" The fourth segment of the detector without rings.
SINGLE "Single" Segment type intended for detectors not divided into multiple segments.
