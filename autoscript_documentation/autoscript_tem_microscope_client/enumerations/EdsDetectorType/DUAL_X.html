EdsDetectorType.DUAL_X

EDS detector with two segments.

Remarks

The Dual-X detectors can be inserted and retracted to protect them. There are some optics modes (like LM and Lorentz) where the detector cannot be inserted. In other cases the detector is inserted automatically when acquisition is started. In case it is necessary to insert the detector before the actual acquisition is started, they can be inserted manually using the insert() function.

To prevent damage, the detectors are automatically retracted when switching the optical_mode, when closing the column valves or when the input count rate is too high.
