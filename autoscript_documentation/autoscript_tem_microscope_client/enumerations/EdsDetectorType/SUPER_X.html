EdsDetectorType.SUPER_X

EDS detector with four segments and a protective shutter.

Remarks

The Super-X detectors have mechanical shutters to protect them. There are some optics modes (like LM and Lorentz) where the shutters cannot be opened. In other cases the shutters are opened automatically when acquisition is started and closed some seconds after the acquisition is finished.

To prevent damage to the detector the shutters are automatically closed when switching the optical_mode, when closing the column valves or when the input count rate is too high.

The SuperX and SuperX G2 variants of the detector have four segments, designated SEGMENT1, SEGMENT2, SEGMENT3, and SEGMENT4. The SuperX G2 Lite variant has only two segments, designated SEGMENT3 and SEGMENT4.
