CameraType.SH_EMPAD

Service hatch mount EMPAD camera.

Remarks

EMPAD (Electron Microscope Pixel Array Detector) cameras are intended for 4D STEM applications and are not supported by the regular TEM camera acquisition functions (acquire_camera_image(), etc.). The 4D STEM acquisition can be triggered by the acquire_stem_data() function.
