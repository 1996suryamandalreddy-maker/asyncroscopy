ApertureMechanismInsertionState

Possible states of an aperture mechanism.

Values

ARBITRARY "Arbitrary" Aperture mechanism is in the arbitrary state. This state is not inserted or retracted, but somewhere in between (free moving alignment mode).
ERROR "Error" Aperture mechanism is reporting a HW error, cannot insert or retract.
INSERTED "Inserted" Aperture mechanism is inserted.
MOVING "Moving" Aperture mechanism is moving, homing or aligning.
RETRACTED "Retracted" Aperture mechanism is retracted.
