optics.scan_field_of_view

The field of view of the scan in meters. Applicable only in STEM mode.

Data type

float

Remarks

The property is only available in STEM optical_mode. Switching between LM and HM modes is not allowed with this property, use objective_lens_mode instead.

Examples


        # Set scan field of view to 9 micrometers
        microscope.optics.scan_field_of_view = 9e-6
