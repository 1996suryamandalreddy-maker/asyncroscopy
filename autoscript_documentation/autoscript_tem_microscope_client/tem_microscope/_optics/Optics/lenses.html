optics.lenses

Provides a direct access to the lenses of the microscope.

Classes

alignments Contains alignments of the lenses in the microscope.

Methods

get_available() Returns the list of available lenses on the microscope. Uses values from LensType.
get_lens() Retrieves a lens by name.
