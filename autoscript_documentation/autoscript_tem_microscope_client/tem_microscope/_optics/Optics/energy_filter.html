optics.energy_filter

Provides access to energy filter (imaging filter) settings.

Classes

apertures Enables control and provides the status of the energy filter apertures.
energy_shift Current of the energy-loss prism.
high_tension_offset Small high tension offset.
mode Enables control and provides the status of the energy filter mode.
slit Enables control and provides the status of the energy filter slit.
