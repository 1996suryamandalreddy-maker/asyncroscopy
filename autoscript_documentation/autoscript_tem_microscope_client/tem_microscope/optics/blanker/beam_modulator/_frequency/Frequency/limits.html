optics.blanker.beam_modulator.frequency.limits

The permissible frequency range of the modulation in Hz.

Data type

[Read only] Limits

Remarks

If a frequency value lays inside the limits, it may not be valid as the combination of frequency and duty_cycle parameters have to adhere to the following rules:

  • The ratio of frequency over duty cycle (the pulse width) has to be greater than minimal pulse step legth.
  • The pulse width pw = duty_cycle / frequency
    • Has to be greater than the minimal pulse step length (10 ns for NanoPulser).
    • The sum of the pulse width and the minimal pulse step length has to be less than the period of the modulation.
