optics.focusing.stem.objective.reset_defocus

Resets objective defocus to 0 (and does not change objective focus).

Syntax

reset_defocus()

Remarks

Objective defocus can be thought of as a relative offset from the focus plane of the objective lens. When you reset the defocus, this offset is cleared, bringing it back to zero. This means that the current position of the condenser lens's focal plane is now considered the new reference point for any future offsets. The total defocus is the sum of the objective defocus and the intensity defocus.

Examples


        # Reset defocus to zero for both intensity and objective defocus and print their values
        microscope.optics.focusing.stem.intensity.reset_defocus()
        microscope.optics.focusing.stem.objective.reset_defocus()

        intensity_focus = microscope.optics.focusing.stem.intensity.focus
        objective_focus = microscope.optics.focusing.stem.objective.focus

        print(f"Intensity defocus: {intensity_focus}")
        print(f"Objective defocus: {objective_focus}")
        print(f"Total defocus: {intensity_focus + objective_focus}")
