AdornedImageMetadataEnergyFilterSettings

Image metadata structure for energy filter values.

Items

acceleration_voltage_offset [Optional] float The offset on the acceleration voltage for Energy Filtered Imaging, in volts.
drift_tube_voltage [Optional] float Drift tube voltage, in volts. Applicable in EELS.
energy_selection_slit_width [Optional] float The width of the energy selection slit, in volts.
energy_shift [Optional] float Energy shift, in volts. Applicable in EELS.
entrance_aperture_diameter [Optional] float Entrance aperture diameter, in meters. Applicable in EELS.
entrance_aperture_type [Optional] str The type of entrance aperture (Circular, Mask).
energy_selection_slit_inserted [Optional] bool Indicates whether the slit is inserted or not.
