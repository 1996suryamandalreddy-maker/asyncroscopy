AdornedImageMetadataGasInjectionSystem

Image metadata structure for gas injection system values.

Items

gases [Optional] List[AdornedImageMetadataGasInjectionSystemGas] List of gases with temperatures in the GIS.
needle_temperature [Optional] float Needle temperature, in Kelvin.
gas_flow_on [Optional] bool Indicates whether gas is flowing.
heater_on [Optional] bool Indicates whether the heater is on.
needle_state [Optional] str The inserted state of the needle: (0 - Invalid, 1 - Offline, 2 - Inserted, 3 - Retracted, 4 - Inserting, 5 - Retracting, 6 - Error, 7 - MaskOnline, 8 - Venting, 9 - Unknown).
port_name [Optional] str The configured GIS port name.
port_number [Optional] int Ordinal number of the GIS port.
custom_properties [Optional] List[AdornedImageMetadataCustomProperties]
custom_sections [Optional] List[AdornedImageMetadataCustomSection]
