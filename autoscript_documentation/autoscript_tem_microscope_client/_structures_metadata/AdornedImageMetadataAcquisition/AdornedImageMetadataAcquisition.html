AdornedImageMetadataAcquisition

Image metadata structure for acquisition values.

Items

acquisition_date_time [Optional] str The time when the result was acquired and the acquisition was completed.
acquisition_id [Optional] str Identifier of the acquisition event, which may have captured more than one result.
acquisition_start_date_time [Optional] str The UTC time when the acquisition started.
beam_type [Optional] str The type of particle used for the primary acquisition of the image.
column_type [Optional] str The type or model of the physical column used for acquisition.
source_type [Optional] str The type of microscope energy source used for imaging.
