EelsExposureSettings

Contains settings for EELS spectrum exposure.

Items

exposure_time float Exposure time in seconds.
drift_tube_energy_offset [Optional] float Drift tube energy offset in electronvolts. Default is 0.

Remarks

The scan engine iterates over the exposure settings at each dwell location. Multiple exposure settings allow to acquire multiple spectra or spectrum image cubes with a single scan. The exposure settings are applied in the order they are defined in the list.
