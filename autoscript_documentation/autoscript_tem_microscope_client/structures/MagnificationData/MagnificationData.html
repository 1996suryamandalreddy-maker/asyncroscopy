MagnificationData

Contains properties of a TEM magnification.

Items

nominal [Read only] float The nominal magnification of the projector.
calibrated [Read only] float The calibrated magnification of the projector.
rotation [Read only] float The rotation of the magnification.
label [Read only] str A label fully describing the magnification in radians.
magnification_sub_mode [Read only] [Optional] str The magnification sub-mode. Values from enumeration MagnificationSubMode are used.
objective_lens_mode [Read only] str The objective lens mode. Values from enumeration ObjectiveLensMode are used.

Remarks

magnification_sub_mode is None when the objective lens mode is LM.
