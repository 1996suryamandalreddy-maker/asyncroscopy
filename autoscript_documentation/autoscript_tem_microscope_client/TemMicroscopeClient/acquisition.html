acquisition

Provides functions for acquisition of TEM and STEM images.

Methods

acquire_camera_image() Acquires an image from a camera.
acquire_camera_image_advanced() Acquires an image from a camera with advanced settings.
acquire_camera_series() Acquires a series of images from a camera.
acquire_camera_series_advanced() Acquires a series of images from a camera with advanced settings.
acquire_stem_data() Triggers a scan task with a given scan synchronizable camera without returning the data.
acquire_stem_data_advanced() Triggers a scan task without returning the data.
acquire_stem_image() Acquires an image using a scanning detector.
acquire_stem_images() Acquires images using multiple scanning detectors.
acquire_stem_images_advanced() Acquires images with advanced settings using specified detectors or segments.
acquire_stem_segment_images() Acquires images from scanning detector segments.
